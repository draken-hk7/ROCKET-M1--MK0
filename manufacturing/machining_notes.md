# Manufacturing Notes

This project describes a non-propellant educational airframe for structural,
aerodynamic, avionics, and controls simulation. The notes below are for benign
airframe and electronics packaging only.

- Body tube: use a commercial composite tube or non-structural display tube.
- Nose cone: 3D print in PETG, or turn from balsa/foam for wind-tunnel display.
- Fins: cut G10 with hand tools, bandsaw, or outsourced laser/waterjet service.
- Voronoi fin cutouts: outsource to a local fabrication shop; wear fiberglass
  dust PPE and wet-sand edges.
- Avionics sled: laser cut 3 mm plywood or print in PETG; use nylon or brass
  PCB standoffs.
- Bulkheads: drill with a press and deburr all edges.
- No CNC is required for the structural education build.
- Do not add pressure-vessel features, combustion chambers, ignition devices,
  energetic deployment circuits, or motor hardware to this repository.
