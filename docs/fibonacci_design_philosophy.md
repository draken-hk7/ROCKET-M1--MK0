# Fibonacci Design Philosophy

ROCKET M1-MK0 embeds biomimetic geometry as a design language for simulation,
layout, and education.

## Background

Natural systems often combine compact packing, gradual curvature, branching,
and cellular structures. Aerospace design frequently borrows similar ideas:
minimum-drag bodies, honeycomb panels, lattice structures, and smoothly varying
planforms. This repository uses those principles visibly and mathematically.

## Vehicle Applications

- Fin sweep: `21 deg x phi = 33.98 deg`.
- Fin span: 89 mm is Fibonacci and pairs with 144 mm for a golden ratio.
- Nose ribs: logarithmic spiral references echo growth spirals.
- Parachute vents: 13 phyllotaxis points distribute holes evenly.
- Avionics bay: 144 mm length follows Fibonacci F(12).
- PCB layout: signal-critical parts follow a Fibonacci spiral priority.
- Sensor fusion: `new/phi^2 + history/phi` creates phi-weighted smoothing.
- Dashboard layout: 61.8/38.2 columns follow the golden split.
